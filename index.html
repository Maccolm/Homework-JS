  • List to choose

    A list of athletes is given. It is necessary to form a list of those who will participate in the competition. There are two columns. One shows all the athletes, the other shows the list of those who were selected. When clicking on the green arrow, the athlete moves to the competition list. When clicking on the red arrow, the athlete moves to the general list.

  • Failing snow

  • Shining stars

    Shining stars. The specified number of stars should appear in a random part of the screen. Each star with a certain step and interval increases from the minimum to the maximum size. Once the maximum size is reached, the star should appear in another random position.
